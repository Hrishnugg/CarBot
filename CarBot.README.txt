CarBot:
-------
CarBot is a chatbot which will give you answers for any car questions you may have! 
It can even give you recommendations for vehicles to purchase based on your price range, features you may want, type of car, etc.
CarBot uses the ChatBison LLM from Google's Vertex.AI.
It is tested on a Google Cloud VM, but it can also run on any other machine with Python3 and the needed packages installed.

Required Packages:
vertexai
google-ai-platform
pysimplegui
colorama
termcolor

Enjoy :)
